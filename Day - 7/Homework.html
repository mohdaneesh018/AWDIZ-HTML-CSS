Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is a highly acclaimed Indian cricketer, celebrated for his aggressive batting style and powerful six-hitting abilities. He is widely recognized as the "Hitman" and has earned a reputation as one of the best white-ball cricketers in the world.
