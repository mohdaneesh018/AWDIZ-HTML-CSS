M S DHONI

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, popularly known as MS Dhoni, is a legendary figure in Indian cricket. He is a right-handed batsman and wicket-keeper, and he served as the captain of the Indian national team in all three formats of the game. Dhoni is known for his exceptional leadership, calm demeanor on the field, and outstanding finishing skills.
